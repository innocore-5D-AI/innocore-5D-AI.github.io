What We Look For

Division 1: Adaptive Robot Platform

  • PhD in Robotics, Mechanical Engineering, or related field
  • Experience in robot control, haptics, or human-robot interaction
  • Skills in sensor/actuator design or embedded systems
  • Background in legged robotics or manipulation systems

Division 2: 5D Robot AI

  • PhD in Computer Science, AI/ML, Robotics, or related field
  • Experience in computer vision, SLAM, or foundation models
  • Skills in deep learning, reinforcement learning
  • Background in robot learning or world modeling
